Floors : G + 1 + Terrace (Ground + First Floor + Roof Terrace)

Architectural Style : Contemporary Modern Residential Architecture

Purpose : To create a premium residential space that blends aesthetics, durability, and sustainable design for modern family living.

  • Modern Architectural Design with Elegant Facade
  • Spacious Balcony & Terrace for Outdoor Living

  • Secure Gated Entry with Stylish Compound Wall
  • Ample Natural Light & Cross Ventilation
  • Integrated Ground-Level Parking Area
  • Outdoor Landscaping to Enhance Aesthetics
  • Flat RCC Terrace with Provision for Solar & Rooftop Garden

Technologies Used

Architectural Design
  • AutoCAD for detailed 2D planning and layouts.
  • SketchUp & 3ds Max for realistic 3D rendering and elevation design.
    •  Structural Engineering
  • RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) framed structure with beams, columns, and slabs.
  • M25 grade concrete with Fe500 TMT reinforcement for strength and seismic safety.
  • Soil-tested foundation for structural stability.
    •  Construction Materials
  • High-quality wire-cut bricks and M-sand mortar.
  • Weather-resistant paints and wooden-textured finishes.
  • Glass balcony railings & UPVC/Aluminium windows for durability.
    •  Plumbing & Electrical
  • Concealed modular wiring with MCBs for safety.
  • Concealed CPVC piping for water distribution and sanitation.
  • Provision for rainwater harvesting and solar power integration.
    •  Sustainability
  • Optimized natural daylight to minimize energy consumption.
  • Terrace garden provision for thermal comfort and eco-friendly living.
  • Energy-efficient lighting and cross-ventilation design.
    •  Safety & Quality Control
  • Steel grill protection on windows for enhanced residential security
  • Ongoing quality inspections during each construction phase for compliance and finish
