House with Shop Construction

Project Overview

This project involves the design and construction of a modern residential + commercial building that integrates a shop on the ground floor with a spacious residence on the upper floor. The project aims to maximize both functional commercial space and comfortable living quarters, following a contemporary architectural style with a focus on durability, aesthetics, and energy efficiency.

Project Type : House + Shop (Mixed-use building)

Location : Arivuthirukovil Road, Dindigul

Floors : G + 1 (Ground + First Floor)

Architectural Style : Contemporary Urban Mixed-Use Architecture

Purpose : A modern mixed-use building combining residential space and commercial shops to serve both living and business

Status : Project Handover

Features

  • Ground Floor - Commercial Space
  • First Floor - Residential Area
  • Independent Access
  • Structural Design
  • Balcony Space
  • Low-Maintenance Exterior

Technologies Used

Architectural Design
  • AutoCAD for precise 2D floor plans
  • SketchUp & 3ds Max for 3D elevation visualization
    •  Structural Engineering
  • RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) columns, beams, and slab system
  • M25 grade concrete with Fe500 TMT bars for durability
  • Soil-tested foundation for load-bearing stability.
    •  Construction Materials
  • High-quality wire-cut bricks and M-sand mortar.
  • Weather-resistant exterior paints and textured finishes.
  • Tempered glass for balcony railings.
    •  Plumbing & Electrical
  • Concealed wiring with modular switches and safety MCBs.
  • CPVC/UPVC pipes for leak-proof water supply and drainage.
  • Provision for solar water heater and rainwater harvesting.
    •  Sustainability
  • Energy-efficient lighting design
  • Ventilation strategy to reduce air-conditioning load.
  • Rainwater harvesting system planned for groundwater recharge.
    •  Safety & Quality Control
  • IS code standards for all construction processes.
  • Site-level quality checks for concrete curing and material strength.
