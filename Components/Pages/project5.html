Residential Structural Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on the design and construction of a multi-story residential building with a modern urban architectural style. The building is designed to offer spacious living areas, energy-efficient planning, and durable structural stability. It integrates large windows for natural lighting, a strong RCC framework, and functional spaces suited for family living.

Project Type :Residential House (Multi-Story Independent Building)

Location : Valakapatty , Dindigul , India

Floors : G + 2 + Terrace (Ground + Two Floors + Roof Terrace)

Architectural Style : Contemporary Urban Residential Architecture

Purpose : To create a durable and comfortable family home with modern amenities and efficient space utilization.

Status : Project Handover

Features

  • Modern Architectural Design
  • Spacious Multi-Level Layout
  • Large Windows for Ample Natural Light
  • RCC Framed Structure for Strength and Stability
  • Provision for Balcony and Terrace Spaces
  • Secure Ground Floor Parking Area
  • Optimized Cross-Ventilation
  • Structural Provision for Future Expansion

Technologies Used

Architectural Design
  • AutoCAD for accurate 2D floor planning.
  • SketchUp & 3ds Max for 3D modeling and elevation visualization.
    •  Structural Engineering
  • RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) framed structure with beams, columns, and slabs.
  • M25 grade concrete with Fe500 TMT bars for durability and seismic safety.
  • Soil-tested foundation to ensure proper load-bearing capacity.
    •  Construction Materials
  • High-quality wire-cut bricks and M-sand mortar.
  • Weather-resistant exterior paints and durable wall finishes.
  • UPVC/Aluminium windows with safety grills and glass panels.
    •  Plumbing & Electrical
  • Concealed modular wiring with MCB protection.
  • Concealed CPVC piping for water distribution and sanitation.
  • Provision for solar water heating and rainwater harvesting.
    •  Sustainability
  • Designed for maximum daylight and ventilation to reduce energy use.
  • Provision for rooftop water harvesting and terrace garden.
  • Energy-efficient lighting and future-ready electrical layout.
    •  Safety & Quality Control
  • Steel grill protection on windows for enhanced residential security
  • Ongoing quality inspections during each construction phase for compliance and finish
